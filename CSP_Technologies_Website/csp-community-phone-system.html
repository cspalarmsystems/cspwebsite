Welcome to CSP Community Phone System

We are a public phone system where you can get your VOIP phone connected with our systems and have fun with your friends!

PSTN Access line: +1 (447) 200-4482

Features:

  • Privacy! None of your calls will be recorded, and all calls you make on your IP phone will be completely private!
  • 3CX App Access, although the main system runs on FreePBX, we can provide you with a 3CX number too!
  • Music Extensions, where you can dial a number to play music! You can request music to be added.
  • Access to TandmX! Dial TandmX numbers from our system! Our TandmX block is 277, also known as the CSP block.
  • Queues! Contact support for setup!
  • Access to UCP!
  • Unlimited extensions for your needs!

Getting Started

Things you will need:

  • A SIP Phone, ATA, or a Softphone app
